Cursor Live in firmware

Controls how hand tilt maps to on-screen cursor movement.

How much a tilt moves the cursor.

0.20
Lower = smoother but laggier. Higher = more responsive.

Clutch & Zoom Live in firmware

Tilt behaviour while the clutch button is held. Cursor tracking is fully off — tilt controls zoom and discrete view commands instead.

20°
Tilt angle before zoom activates.
250 ms
Time between zoom ticks. Higher = slower zoom.
15°
Tilt needed to trigger Zoom-fit or View-normal.
150 ms
How long to hold the tilt before it fires.
5°
Must return within this angle before firing again. Keep below fire angle.
Invert clutch roll Swap Zoom-fit / View-normal directions.
Invert zoom Swap zoom in / zoom out direction.
Clutch Macros Live in firmware

Remap clutch-mode actions without reflashing. Separate keys with + — e.g. ctrl+z. Modifiers: ctrl, shift, alt, gui/cmd/win. Zoom direction (tilt left/right) is not remappable here.

On Mac, swap ctrl+y → cmd+shift+z for Redo. Hold threshold is 600ms. Changes apply on next Send.
